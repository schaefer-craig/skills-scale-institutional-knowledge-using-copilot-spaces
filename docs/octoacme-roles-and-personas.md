# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers focus on the user experience, ensuring that products are intuitive, accessible, and delightful. They collaborate with Product Managers, Developers, and stakeholders to create user-centered designs and validate them through research and testing.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and usability testing to inform design decisions
- Create wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity mockups
- Define user flows, interaction patterns, and information architecture
- Collaborate with Developers to ensure designs are implemented accurately
- Maintain design systems and component libraries
- Advocate for accessibility and inclusive design practices

### Goals
- Improve user satisfaction and task completion rates
- Reduce friction and confusion in user journeys
- Ensure consistent, accessible experiences across products
- Balance user needs with technical and business constraints

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews with Product Managers and stakeholders
- Collaboration sessions with Developers during implementation
- User research findings and usability test reports
- Design specs and handoff documentation

### Integration Points
- **Product Managers**: Collaborate on feature definition, validate designs align with product goals
- **Developers**: Partner on implementation feasibility, provide design assets and specifications
- **QA Lead**: Work together on acceptance criteria that include UX/UI validation
- **Customer Success**: Gather user feedback and pain points to inform design improvements

---

## Quality Assurance Lead

### Role Summary
Quality Assurance Leads ensure that products meet acceptance criteria, quality standards, and user expectations. They define testing strategies, coordinate test execution, and advocate for quality throughout the development lifecycle.

### Responsibilities
- Develop comprehensive test plans and strategies
- Design and execute manual and automated test cases
- Identify, document, and track defects through resolution
- Validate acceptance criteria and Definition of Done
- Coordinate regression testing and release validation
- Champion quality metrics and continuous improvement

### Goals
- Prevent defects from reaching production
- Maintain high test coverage and clear quality signals
- Reduce regression and post-release issues
- Improve team testing practices and efficiency

### Typical Communication
- Test plan reviews with Project Managers and Developers
- Daily standup updates on testing progress and blockers
- Bug triage sessions and defect reports
- Release readiness assessments and go/no-go recommendations

### Integration Points
- **Developers**: Collaborate on testability, review test cases, coordinate bug fixes
- **Project Managers**: Report on quality status, risks, and release readiness
- **UX Designer**: Validate that user experience matches design specifications
- **DevOps Engineer**: Integrate automated tests into CI/CD pipelines

---

## DevOps Engineer

### Role Summary
DevOps Engineers build and maintain the infrastructure, tooling, and automation that enable reliable, efficient software delivery. They bridge development and operations, focusing on CI/CD, observability, and operational excellence.

### Responsibilities
- Design and maintain CI/CD pipelines and deployment automation
- Manage infrastructure, cloud resources, and configuration
- Implement monitoring, logging, and alerting systems
- Optimize build times, deployment processes, and resource usage
- Respond to incidents and implement reliability improvements
- Ensure security best practices in infrastructure and pipelines

### Goals
- Enable fast, safe deployments with minimal manual intervention
- Maintain high system availability and performance
- Provide clear observability into system health and behavior
- Reduce toil through automation and self-service tooling

### Typical Communication
- Infrastructure and deployment planning with Developers
- Incident response coordination and post-mortems
- Capacity planning discussions with Project Managers
- Security and compliance reviews with stakeholders

### Integration Points
- **Developers**: Provide deployment pipelines, troubleshoot build/deploy issues
- **QA Lead**: Integrate automated tests into CI/CD, provide test environments
- **Project Managers**: Report on deployment status, coordinate release windows
- **Data Analyst**: Ensure data pipelines and analytics infrastructure are operational

---

## Data Analyst

### Role Summary
Data Analysts turn data into actionable insights that inform product decisions, measure success, and identify opportunities. They collaborate with Product Managers and stakeholders to define metrics, build dashboards, and analyze trends.

### Responsibilities
- Define and track key performance indicators (KPIs) and success metrics
- Build and maintain dashboards and reporting systems
- Conduct exploratory analysis to identify trends and opportunities
- Validate hypotheses through A/B tests and experiments
- Collaborate on data collection and instrumentation requirements
- Communicate findings through visualizations and presentations

### Goals
- Enable data-driven decision making across the organization
- Provide timely, accurate insights into product and business performance
- Identify opportunities for optimization and growth
- Ensure data quality and accessibility

### Typical Communication
- Metric reviews with Product Managers and stakeholders
- Data requirements discussions with Developers
- Analysis presentations and insight reports
- Dashboard reviews and metric definition workshops

### Integration Points
- **Product Managers**: Define success metrics, analyze feature impact, inform roadmap decisions
- **Developers**: Specify instrumentation and data collection requirements
- **DevOps Engineer**: Coordinate on data pipeline infrastructure and monitoring
- **Customer Success**: Analyze customer behavior patterns and satisfaction trends

---

## Customer Success Manager

### Role Summary
Customer Success Managers ensure that customers achieve their desired outcomes with the product. They act as the voice of the customer, gathering feedback, coordinating support escalations, and advocating for customer needs in product development.

### Responsibilities
- Build and maintain relationships with key customers
- Onboard new customers and ensure successful adoption
- Gather customer feedback and identify common pain points
- Coordinate escalations and ensure timely resolution
- Track customer health metrics and proactively address risks
- Advocate for customer needs in roadmap and prioritization discussions

### Goals
- Maximize customer satisfaction and retention
- Drive product adoption and feature utilization
- Reduce churn and identify expansion opportunities
- Ensure customer feedback influences product direction

### Typical Communication
- Regular check-ins and business reviews with customers
- Feedback summaries and feature requests to Product Managers
- Escalation coordination with support and engineering teams
- Customer health reports and success metrics

### Integration Points
- **Product Managers**: Share customer insights, prioritize feature requests, beta testing coordination
- **UX Designer**: Provide user feedback and usability issues from customers
- **Developers**: Coordinate on bug reports and feature clarifications
- **Data Analyst**: Review customer usage data and engagement metrics
